Projects I have SpearHeaded

ROAM: Revolutionizing Email Marketing Dynamics

At Ringier One Africa Media, I spearheaded email automation and personalization as a Digital Marketer. The goal was to enhance open rates, click-through rates (CTRs), and streamline the laborious task of curating and scheduling numerous emails. Collaborating with my manager, we devised various strategies and collaborated with developers to integrate website tags. These tags synchronized with our email system, allowing us to tailor newsletters based on users' preferences in real-time.

Witnessing this initiative evolve and witnessing the gradual improvement in email performance metrics was truly fascinating. The success of this project, accomplished within roughly four months, underscored the pivotal role of email newsletters for ROAM.

Dynamic Digital Campaign: Relaunching The Standard Newspaper

The Standard newspaper, a long-standing print in Kenya, underwent a big change— a re-brand. When I joined the Standard Group, I took on a major project: creating a digital plan to make people aware of the updated newspaper and boost its sales. The idea was to make the paper look better and introduce different themed sections each day—like Financials on Tuesday, Real Estate on Thursday, The Pulse on Fridays, and Gen X on Sundays.

These sections aimed to attract readers with different interests on different days. My digital strategy included teasers, throwback posters of the newspaper, and social media quizzes to excite people leading up to the launch. The build-up phase went well. I collaborated with Twitter influencers to make #TheStandardRelaunch a top trending topic for three days straight. On social media, I shared bits of the day's story to encourage people to buy the paper while gradually revealing its fresh new look.I also organized events on the ground, ran TV commercials, formed partnerships, and aired radio ads to support the rebrand. This was a continuous effort and we gradually saw uptake of the print take-up as advertisers increased as well.

YouTube-Centric Strategy: KTN News' Resurgence Journey

KTN News underwent a significant transformation in 2022. Despite stagnant viewership, the station aimed to revamp its image and attract new audiences and advertisers by rebranding and focusing on news coverage around the clock, akin to a Kenyan version of CNN. The transformation involved setting up a cutting-edge digital studio to enhance the viewing experience and streamline anchor coordination during breaking news. My role in this fascinating project centered on overseeing digital initiatives, particularly on YouTube, given its popularity among Kenyan content consumers.

Following a strategy similar to The Standard's approach, I curated content for teasers, and throwback posts, and engaged users through trivia contests and giveaways on social media. I collaborated closely with past and present journalists, sharing their memories and support for the station's evolution through video messages. After a grand launch event, we hit the road with shows and events to increase viewership. The result? A notable month-on-month increase of 15% in viewership, marking a successful turnaround for KTN News.
